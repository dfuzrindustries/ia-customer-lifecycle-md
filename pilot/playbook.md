# PILOT 60-Day Playbook

**Standardized playbook for conducting PILOT engagements**

---

**Version:** 1.0  
**Audience:** IA Engagement Teams  
**Phase:** Production Pilot  

---

## PILOT Team RACI Key

IA Team Roles:

R= Responsible |A= Accountable |C= Consulted |I= Informed

Team Members:PM (Product Manager), DE (Data Engineer), AI (AI Solutions Architect), CSM (Customer Success Manager), OPS (Operations Lead), CEO (Client Executive Sponsor)

PILOT phase requires production deployment expertise and operational readiness.

### Play 7 ‚Äî Days 28-36

**Expanded Training & Deployment**

Train and deploy to 60-80% of target user base.

#### Training Approaches

- Large group webinars (50-100 people)
- Train-the-trainer (recommended)
- Office hours for ongoing support

### Play 8 ‚Äî Days 37-40

**Phase 2 Assessment**

Evaluate expansion success; refine support processes.

### Play 10 ‚Äî Days 49-55

**ROI Measurement & Documentation**

Quantify business impact; document operational playbooks.

#### ROI Components

- Time savings calculation
- Cost reduction analysis
- Quality improvement metrics
- 3-year projection

### Play 11 ‚Äî Days 56-58

**Executive Presentation Preparation**

Create compelling presentation synthesizing 60 days of work.

#### Presentation Structure (30-40 slides)

- Executive summary & ROI
- Pilot results with data
- User testimonials
- Year 1 transformation roadmap
- Investment & decision required

### Play 12 ‚Äî Days 59-60

**Executive Readout & Year 1 Kickoff**

Present results; secure Year 1 approval; celebrate success.

#### Possible Outcomes

- Year 1 Approved: Begin 4-cycle transformation program
- Partial Approval: Scale current solutions
- Extend Pilot: Gather more data
- Success Without Scale: Client manages internally
