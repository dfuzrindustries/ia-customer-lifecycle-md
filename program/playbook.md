# Program: Year 1 Playbook

**Standardized playbook for Year 1 transformation programs**

---

**Version:** 1.0  
**Audience:** IA Engagement Teams  
**Phase:** Year 1 Transformation  

---

## Program Team RACI Key

IA Team Roles:

R= Responsible |A= Accountable |C= Consulted |I= Informed

Team Members:PGM (Program Manager), AI (AI Solutions Architect), CSM (Customer Success Manager), CO (Change Officer), CEO (Client Executive Sponsor), FL (Functional Leads)

Year 1 transformation requires program management and organization-wide change leadership.

### Play 2 ‚Äî Days 16-40

**IntelligentOS Platform Deployment**

Deploy core IntelligentOS platform, establish data infrastructure, and implement KPI tracking system.

#### Technical Implementation

- Platform infrastructure provisioning (cloud, databases, security)
- System integrations (CRM, HRIS, ERP, Product Analytics, etc.)
- Data pipeline development and testing
- KPI dashboard implementation
- User authentication and access control

#### Deliverables

- IntelligentOS platform operational
- Core systems integrated
- Real-time KPI dashboards
- Technical documentation

### Play 3 ‚Äî Days 41-70

**Priority Function AI Deployment**

Deploy initial AI agents and automated workflows in 3-4 priority functions.

Typically: Sales (forecasting), Marketing (campaign optimization), Finance (reporting), IT (incident management)

#### Function Selection

#### Implementation Per Function

- Workflow mapping and automation design
- AI agent configuration and training
- User training and change management
- Pilot deployment and monitoring
- Feedback incorporation and refinement

#### Deliverables

- 3-4 functions at Level 2 maturity
- Initial AI agents operational
- Documented ROI from pilot functions

### Play 4 ‚Äî Days 71-90

**Cycle 1 Assessment & Planning**

Assess Cycle 1 outcomes, measure ROI, plan Cycle 2 expansion.

#### Assessment Activities

- Maturity progression validation (Level 1 ‚Üí Level 2)
- ROI measurement and documentation
- Lessons learned capture
- Cycle 2 detailed planning
- Executive briefing and approval

#### Success Criteria

- 3-4 functions demonstrating Level 2 maturity
- 10-15% efficiency gains measured
- Platform stable and scalable
- Executive approval for Cycle 2

### Play 6 ‚Äî Days 116-145

**Cross-Functional Process Orchestration**

Implement cross-functional workflows; enable process orchestration across functions.

#### Integration Focus

- Lead-to-cash automation (Marketing ‚Üí Sales ‚Üí Finance)
- Hire-to-retire workflows (HR ‚Üí IT ‚Üí Finance)
- Concept-to-customer (Product ‚Üí Marketing ‚Üí Sales ‚Üí Delivery)
- Plan-to-produce (Strategy ‚Üí Product ‚Üí Delivery)

#### Deliverables

- 4+ cross-functional workflows operational
- Process orchestration engine deployed
- Measurable handoff efficiency improvements

### Play 7 ‚Äî Days 146-170

**OKR Alignment & KPI Integration**

Align company-wide OKRs; achieve cross-functional KPI alignment; implement quarterly business reviews.

#### Alignment Activities

- Company OKR framework definition
- Functional OKR cascading
- KPI-to-OKR mapping
- Quarterly business review process

#### Deliverables

- OKR system operational
- KPI dashboards linked to OKRs
- QBR process established
- All functions at Level 3 maturity

### Play 8 ‚Äî Days 171-180

**Cycle 2 Assessment & Planning**

Validate Level 3 maturity; measure expansion ROI; plan Cycle 3 integration.

#### Success Criteria

- All 9 functions at Level 3
- 15-20% efficiency gains organization-wide
- Cross-functional workflows operational
- OKR system driving decisions

### Play 10 ‚Äî Days 211-240

**End-to-End Autonomous Processes**

Enable fully autonomous business processes; implement continuous improvement loops.

#### Autonomous Capabilities

- Autonomous forecasting (Sales + Finance)
- Autonomous campaign optimization (Marketing)
- Autonomous incident resolution (IT)
- Autonomous hiring workflows (HR + IT)
- Autonomous customer success interventions (Accounts)

#### Deliverables

- 5+ fully autonomous processes
- Human-in-loop only for exceptions
- Continuous learning operational

### Play 11 ‚Äî Days 241-260

**Economic Impact Optimization**

Optimize all processes for maximum ROI; implement predictive intelligence.

#### Optimization Focus

- Cost optimization across all functions
- Revenue acceleration initiatives
- Quality improvement programs
- Predictive analytics deployment

#### Deliverables

- 25-30% efficiency gains validated
- Cost reduction documented
- Revenue acceleration measured
- All functions at Level 4 maturity

### Play 12 ‚Äî Days 261-270

**Cycle 3 Assessment & Planning**

Validate Level 4 maturity; prepare for final transformation cycle.

#### Success Criteria

- All functions at Level 4
- Autonomous decision-making operational
- 25-30% efficiency gains achieved
- Ready for Level 5 transformation

### Play 14 ‚Äî Days 301-330

**Cultural Transformation Completion**

Complete organizational transformation; embed AI-native culture; achieve Level 5 maturity.

#### Cultural Initiatives

- Leadership transformation (AI-native decision-making)
- Organization-wide AI fluency
- Innovation culture embedded
- Continuous improvement mindset

#### Deliverables

- All 9 functions at Level 5 maturity
- Organization operates as agentic enterprise
- 40%+ efficiency gains validated

### Play 15 ‚Äî Days 331-350

**Comprehensive ROI Documentation**

Document complete transformation journey; measure and validate full ROI.

#### Documentation

- Comprehensive ROI analysis
- Before/after comparison across all metrics
- Case studies and success stories
- Lessons learned and best practices

#### Deliverables

- Year 1 transformation report
- ROI validation across all functions
- Executive presentation (board-ready)

### Play 16 ‚Äî Days 351-365

**Partnership Transition**

Transition from transformation to ongoing partnership; plan future innovation cycles.

#### Transition Activities

- Capability transfer and sustainability planning
- Partnership model selection
- Future roadmap development
- Executive celebration and recognition

#### Partnership Options

- Continuous innovation cycles
- Co-investment model (IA Fund)
- Managed services
- Independent operation with support

#### Deliverables

- Partnership agreement executed
- Year 2+ roadmap approved
- Celebration event completed
- Level 5 agentic enterprise certified
