# Program: Year 1 Roadmap

**Transformation Program — Four 90-Day Cycles**

---

**Duration:** 12 months  
**Investment:** $600,000  
**Objective:** Enterprise-wide AI transformation  

---

## Program Purpose

The Year 1 Transformation Program systematically elevates your organization from operational maturity Level 1 (Ad Hoc) to Level 5 (AI-Native Enterprise) across all 9 business functions. Through four structured 90-day cycles, we transform CEO/Strategy, Product, Marketing, Sales, Accounts/CSM, Delivery/Operations, HR/People, IT, and Finance into autonomous, AI-powered functions.

By Day 365, your organization operates as an agentic enterprise where AI agents autonomously execute workflows, make decisions, coordinate across functions, learn continuously, and drive measurable business outcomes without human intervention in routine processes.

- Level 5 maturity achievedacross all 9 functions
- AI agents deployedfor CEO, Product, Marketing, Sales, Accounts, Delivery, HR, IT, Finance
- Measurable ROIdemonstrated: ≥40% operational efficiency gain, ≥30% cost reduction, ≥25% revenue acceleration
- Full IntelligentOS deploymentwith unified KPI architecture and cross-functional orchestration

- Self-sustaining capabilityto continue innovation independently
