# Program — Year One Transformation


**Enterprise-wide AI transformation through four 90-day cycles**

---

## Overview

- **Duration:** 12 months (four 90-day cycles)
- **Investment:** $600,000
- **Format:** Continuous transformation program
- **Team:** Dedicated transformation team

---

## Documents

- **[Agenda](agenda.md)** — Client-facing program roadmap
- **[Playbook](playbook.md)** — Internal operations guide

---

## Purpose

Enterprise-wide AI transformation through four consecutive 90-day cycles that progressively transform the organization into a truly agentic enterprise.

**Four Cycles:**
1. **Foundation** — Prove & validate core capabilities
2. **Expansion** — Scale & refine across functions
3. **Integration** — Optimize & embed into operations
4. **Transformation** — Enterprise-wide AI adoption (Level 5 maturity)

---

## What You'll Walk Away With

✓ **Agentic Enterprise Status** — Level 5 AI maturity across the organization  
✓ **Enterprise-Wide Deployment** — AI solutions embedded in critical workflows across all major functions  
✓ **AI Center of Excellence** — Internal team capable of sustaining and expanding AI initiatives  
✓ **Measured Business Impact** — Documented ROI across multiple business units with quantified value  
✓ **Operating Model** — Governance, processes, and playbooks for ongoing AI innovation  
✓ **Technology Foundation** — Scalable architecture and infrastructure for future AI initiatives  
✓ **Change Management Framework** — Proven adoption strategies and organizational alignment  
✓ **Innovation Pipeline** — Identified next-generation opportunities and strategic roadmap

---

## Key Activities

### Cycle 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)
**Focus:** Prove value and establish foundation
- Deploy 2-3 high-value AI solutions to production
- Establish AI governance and operating model
- Build initial AI Center of Excellence team
- Document early ROI and success stories
- Identify expansion opportunities across functions

### Cycle 2: Expansion (Months 4-6)
**Focus:** Scale proven solutions across functions
- Expand successful solutions to additional business units
- Deploy 3-5 new AI capabilities in key workflows
- Scale AI Center of Excellence with internal champions
- Develop cross-functional integration strategies
- Build organizational AI literacy and adoption

### Cycle 3: Integration (Months 7-9)
**Focus:** Optimize and embed AI into operations
- Integrate AI solutions with core enterprise systems
- Optimize performance and refine based on usage data
- Establish self-service AI capabilities for power users
- Develop advanced automation and agentic workflows
- Create sustainable support and maintenance model

### Cycle 4: Transformation (Months 10-12)
**Focus:** Achieve enterprise-wide AI maturity
- Complete enterprise-wide deployment of core AI capabilities
- Establish ongoing innovation pipeline and experimentation
- Transition to self-sustaining AI operations
- Achieve Level 5 Agentic Enterprise maturity
- Define partnership model for continued innovation
