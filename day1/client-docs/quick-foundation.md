# Day 1 Workshop — Quick Reference  
## Data & AI Foundation

## Objective
Assess organizational readiness for automation by evaluating data, decision, and operating foundations.

---

## Duration
One working session (approximately 6 hours).

---

## Participants
Senior leaders responsible for:
- Data and analytics
- Technology
- Operations
- Finance

---

## Key Outputs
- Data and AI maturity assessment
- Identified foundational constraints
- Prioritized improvement roadmap
- Guidance on automation readiness

---

## Success Criteria
The workshop is successful if it:
- Clarifies current limitations
- Aligns leadership on priorities
- Establishes a credible, sequenced path forward
