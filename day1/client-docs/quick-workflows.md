# Day 1 Workshop — Quick Reference  
## Automated Workflows

## Objective
Identify and prioritize high-value workflows suitable for automation and near-term economic impact.

---

## Duration
One working session (approximately 6 hours).

---

## Participants
Senior leaders with responsibility for:
- Operations
- Revenue
- Finance
- Product or Service Delivery

---

## Key Outputs
- 1-3 prioritized workflows
- Initial economic impact estimates
- Automation readiness assessment
- Recommended next phase

---

## Success Criteria
The workshop is successful if it produces:
- Clear, actionable priorities
- Alignment on what to pursue and what not to pursue
- A shared understanding of constraints, risks, and tradeoffs
