## Overview

- **Duration:** 6 hours
- **Investment:** $25,000
- **Format:** Facilitated in-person or virtual workshop
- **Participants:** C-suite executives, functional leads, key stakeholders

---

## Documents

- **[Agenda](agenda.md)** — Client-facing workshop brief
- **[Playbook](playbook.md)** — Internal facilitation guide for IA teams

---

## Purpose

The Day 1 Workshop is a structured, facilitated session designed to identify 1-3 high-value workflow redesign opportunities that will either save money or make money. We assess organizational readiness and provide a clear view of current state alongside a recommended path to measurable business impact.

---

## What You'll Walk Away With

1. **Clear AI Opportunities** — 1-3 prioritized POC recommendations
2. **Value Assessment** — Estimated ROI and business impact for each opportunity
3. **Readiness Snapshot** — Current state assessment across people, process, technology
4. **Statement of Work** — Concrete 30-day POC scope and timeline

---

## Key Activities

- Business Canvas: Strategic mapping and AI readiness assessment
- Operational Canvas: Value stream mapping and maturity assessment
- Workflow prioritization using Impact × Feasibility framework
- POC scoping with defined success metrics
- SOW development for 30-day POC phase
