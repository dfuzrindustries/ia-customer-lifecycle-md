# Partnership Playbook
**Standardized playbook for Partnership engagements**

---

**Version:** 1.0  
**Audience:** IA Engagement Teams  
**Phase:** Partnership  

---

## Partnership Team RACI Key

IA Team Roles:

R= Responsible |A= Accountable |C= Consulted |I= Informed

Team Members:BD (Business Development), AI (AI Solutions Architect), FIN (Finance Lead), LEGAL (Legal Counsel), CEO (Client Executive Sponsor), PGM (Program Manager)

Partnership phase involves co-investment structure and joint venture governance.

**Rapid Prototyping & Validation**

Execute rapid build-measure-learn cycles within 90-day window.

Explicitly define when to stop investing in an opportunity:

#### Build-Measure-Learn Approach

- Sprint 1 (Weeks 1-3):Prototype core hypothesis
- Sprint 2 (Weeks 4-6):User testing and feedback
- Sprint 3 (Weeks 7-9):Refinement based on learnings
- Sprint 4 (Weeks 10-12):Scale readiness or pivot decision

#### Validation Methods

- Customer interviews (minimum 20 target users)
- Prototype testing (working demo, not slideware)
- Economic modeling (unit economics must work)
- Technical validation (can we build and scale this?)

#### Kill Criteria

#### Deliverables

- Working prototype or MVP
- Validation data (customer, technical, economic)
- Lessons learned documentation
- Go/no-go recommendation with data

**Fund Partnership Execution & Monitoring**

Execute IA Fund partnerships with proper governance and performance tracking.

Plan exit strategy from inception:

#### Execution Framework

- Dedicated Team:IA commits full-time resources (2-5 people depending on scope)
- Capital Deployment:Phased funding tied to milestones
- Regular Reporting:Monthly financials, quarterly board reviews
- Performance Metrics:Track against projections, adjust as needed

#### Risk Management

- Monthly risk assessment and mitigation planning
- Early warning indicators for off-track performance
- Pivot or exit criteria clearly defined
- Regular strategic reviews (quarterly)

#### Exit Planning

#### Deliverables

- Monthly performance dashboards
- Quarterly board presentations
- Annual strategic plan updates
- Exit readiness assessment (ongoing)

**Continuous Improvement Program**

Drive ongoing improvements in AI agent performance and business outcomes.

#### Improvement Framework

- Agent Retraining:Quarterly retraining cycles with new data
- Workflow Optimization:Identify and eliminate bottlenecks
- Feature Enhancements:Add capabilities based on user requests
- Integration Expansion:Connect new systems as needed

#### Performance Tracking

- Track KPIs monthly (compare to baseline and targets)
- User satisfaction surveys (quarterly)
- Agent accuracy metrics (continuous)
- Business impact measurement (ROI validation)

#### Deliverables

- Quarterly improvement report
- Agent performance dashboards
- Annual ROI validation

**Strategic Advisory Retainer**

Provide ongoing strategic guidance without operational involvement.

$10K - $15K per quarter (optional)

#### Retainer Scope

- Quarterly Strategic Sessions:4-hour working sessions on strategic topics
- On-Demand Consultation:Ad hoc questions via email/call (up to 5 hours/quarter)
- Annual Platform Review:Full assessment and roadmap update
- Priority Access:First-in-line if client needs more help

#### Typical Topics

- New AI capability exploration
- Platform scaling questions
- Vendor selection and RFPs
- Team hiring and organization design
- Strategic planning and roadmapping

#### Investment

#### Deliverables

- Quarterly session summaries
- Annual strategic assessment
- Ad hoc recommendations as requested
